Unhealthy Eating

Unhealthy eating means eating too much junk food and not enough healthy food. Junk food usually has: Too much sugar (like candy and soda) Too much fat (like fried foods and chips) Too much salt (like processed snacks) Not enough vitamins and fiber These foods taste good but are not good for your body.
It is important to maintain a balanced diet that includes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in order to stay healthy and energized.

Style Attribute Explanation

Below is a list of the style attributes we used and what they do:

  • color – changes the text color.
  • background-color – changes the background color of an element.
  • font-size – controls how big or small the text looks.
  • text-align – aligns the text to the left, right, or center.
  • padding – adds space inside the element, around the content.
  • border – adds a border around the element.
